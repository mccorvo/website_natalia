# SEO research brief — articles 35–44 deep batch

## Editorial objective

This tranche moves deeper than general diet topics and targets Polish patient-intent queries that usually appear when a person has a test, diagnosis, persistent symptom or deficiency question. The set deliberately avoids duplicating articles 25–34 and focuses on high-use practical searches: colonoscopy preparation, diverticular disease, post-cholecystectomy eating, pancreatitis, histamine intolerance, migraine, psoriasis, vitamin D, vitamin B12 and haemorrhoids.

## Selection logic

The topics were selected using a Poland-first proxy: official Polish coverage, clinical actionability, patient safety boundaries and likelihood of search phrasing such as `co jeść`, `czego unikać`, `dieta przy...`, `jadłospis`, `po zabiegu`, `niedobór`. The strongest institutional anchors are NCEZ/PZH, Pacjent.gov.pl, NFZ/Diety NFZ and current international clinical or professional guidance where needed.
